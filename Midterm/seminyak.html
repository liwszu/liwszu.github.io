Seminyak

Potato Head

After our hike we took a taxi to Seminyak for Michelle's last night. The Capsule Hostel was our first stop so we could leave our bags. Darby realized she lost her flipflops and had to buy a pair from one of the street vendors. They saw she had no shoes so they refused to sell them for less than five US dollars which she was not happy about. They did help her walk a half hour to Potato Head Beach Club where we met Bex. The beach club had a beautiful infinity pool that we soaked in for a couple hours. We all agreed that we couldn't leave without a picture, so I asked a group of guys if any of them could take our picture and they did. Funny enough, they were staying in our hostel. We all met up when we returned the hostel and decided to stay in that night since it was monsooning. It turned out to be a very wholesome night full of bonding and sharing life stories. And Jack showed us how he can fit his fist in his mouth... Seminyak was too touristy for us and we decided we wanted to be surrounded by backpackers in smaller towns.
